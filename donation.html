Blood & Organ Donation Policies

  • Blood Donation: You must be between 18–65 years of age and weigh at least 50kg.
  • You can donate whole blood every 3 months.
  • Ensure you have eaten well and are hydrated before donation.
  • Bring a valid ID card and any medical documents if applicable.
  • Timings: Monday to Saturday, 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM
  • Walk-ins are accepted, but appointments are preferred for better service.

Organ Donation Information

  • Organ donation can be done either as a living donor (kidney, liver segment) or posthumously (heart, lungs, etc.).
  • Consent forms and family consultation are mandatory for all types of donation.
  • Our center follows strict government and WHO guidelines for all organ transplants.
  • Timings for consultation: Monday to Friday, 10:00 AM – 4:00 PM
  • For more details, contact our Organ Donation Coordinator listed on the Contact Info page.
